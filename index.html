Our Mission

Reaching A Generation
Endangered

R.A.G.E. Ministries is a non-profit dedicated to preaching the Gospel to the next generation and equipping them to do the same. Your support enables us to serve in churches across the country, develop music and resources, train young leaders in evangelism, and expand our digital reach.

By using my gifts in teaching, equipping, and music, I help connect with youth for the sake of the Gospel. Every donation — whether one-time, monthly, or through sponsoring resources — directly fuels our mission to reach more young people with the hope of Christ. Thank you for partnering with us!

In Christ, Zeke
